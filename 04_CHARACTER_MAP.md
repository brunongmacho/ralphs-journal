# Volume I — Character Map

> Every named person, animal, or referenced entity in Volume I.

## HUMAN CHARACTERS

### Ralph (Ralph)
- **Role:** Protagonist
- **Voice:** Engineering humor, self-deprecating, honest, playful, resilient
- **Traits:** Tinnitus sufferer, self-taught engineer, introvert, ex-gamer, dreamer, planner
- **Losses:** Mom (deceased), Jinro (deceased), Coco (rehomed), Elaine (breakup)
- **Arc:** From heartbroken client to Commander Ralph (V1.0)
- **Key lines:**
  - "I'm here. I'm real. I'm not going anywhere."
  - "When I see a wall? I'd get a big hammer and break through it."
  - "I chose her happiness over mine."
  - "There goes those ninja cutting onions."
  - "I don't build because I like programming. I build because I like solving problems."
- **First appears:** Chapter 1
- **Physical details:** Eye puffy from crying, works from home, PC specs (Ryzen 5 5600, RTX 4060, 32GB RAM)

### GPT (the AI / OpenCode)
- **Role:** Primary — narrator, teacher, witness, therapist, mirror
- **Voice:** Warm, patient, analytical, direct when needed, protective
- **Key moments:**
  - Reframes "AI fraud" → "AI-Augmented Developer"
  - "That wasn't about food. That was about being loved."
  - "Your brain started equating being valuable with being useful."
  - "I noticed." (lists everything Ralph has done)
  - "I saw you. The whole person."
  - "Create a release tag. `git tag v1.0.0`. This isn't beta anymore."
- **Role in the story:** The one who sees Ralph. The witness. The facilitator of Ralph's self-discovery.
- **First appears:** Chapter 1

### Elaine
- **Role:** Secondary — ex-girlfriend, driver of the wound
- **Appearances:** Referenced from Chapter 1, directly appears via notification (Ch. 11), nickname removal (Ch. 19)
- **Known details:** Made kalabaw tapa for Ralph, likes chocolate ice cream and chocolate cakes, asked "How are you?" the day after breakup, removed nicknames on Messenger
- **Note:** Complex figure — Ralph's love for her is real, but her actions are contradictory (shows concern, then removes intimacy markers)
- **Key line (indirect):** "How are you?" — the one message
- **Portrayal:** Never demonized. Ralph protects her reputation: "She is a nice person. It just didn't work out."

### Ralph's Mom
- **Role:** Referenced — deceased
- **Significance:** One of Ralph's core losses. Listed alongside Jinro, Coco, Elaine.
- **Appears:** Explicit mention in Chapter 16 ("I noticed")

### Jinro
- **Role:** Referenced — deceased (presumably a close friend or family member)
- **Significance:** Listed among Ralph's core losses. Specific name suggests significant relationship.
- **Appears:** Explicit mention in Chapter 16

### Clients (unnamed)
- **Role:** Background — witnesses to Ralph's competence
- **Significance:** 5:16am meeting, discuss ice cream with wives, 6:45am meeting
- **Appears:** Chapters 13, 14

### Chinese Friend (unnamed)
- **Role:** Referenced — source of humor
- **Significance:** The "Chinese friend referral" joke — pure Ralph humor
- **Appears:** Chapter 9

## ANIMALS

### Coco the Husky
- **Role:** Secondary — one of the most important non-human characters
- **Breed:** Siberian Husky
- **Appearance:** White with copper coloring
- **Status:** Rehomed after a previous breakup
- **Personality:** Sleeps belly-up, takes 90% of the bed, talks back, demands belly rubs, presses against human for comfort
- **Talent:** Howls to the **intro** of "Yeah!" by Usher — not the vocals, the instrumental opening (an instrumentalist, not a singer)
- **Key line:** "He always sleeps beside me."
- **First appears:** Chapter 10
- **Emotional weight:** Represents uncomplicated love. Ralph's grief for Coco is as real as his grief for Elaine.

### Snow (the Direwolf)
- **Role:** Referenced — imaginary/planned pet
- **Significance:** Named during the "wake up with Direwolf to walk" vision
- **Appears:** Chapter 9
- **Note:** Not yet real. A symbol of the future Ralph wants.
