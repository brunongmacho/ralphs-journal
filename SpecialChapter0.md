# SPECIAL CHAPTER 0

# To My Future Someone

---

If you're reading this...

then somewhere along the way, our paths crossed.

I don't know how we met.

I don't know how old we were.

I don't know if we found each other quickly...

or after years of living separate lives.

As I write these words, you don't exist in my story yet.

At least...

not in any way that I know.

And maybe that's what makes this chapter special.

It's the only one in this journal written for someone I've never met.

✦

Before you continue reading...

there's something I hope you understand.

These pages are not here because I couldn't let go of my past.

They're here because I chose not to erase it.

There's a difference.

Every chapter you'll read was written while I was becoming the man you're about to know.

Some pages were written through tears.

Some through laughter.

Some over coffee.

Some after long nights when sleep refused to come.

Every one of them is real.

I left them untouched because pretending they never happened would be dishonest.

And if we're going to build a life together...

I want honesty to be where we begin.

✦

You'll probably meet people inside these pages.

Some stayed.

Some left.

Some taught me lessons they never knew they were teaching.

You'll meet Elaine.

I hope you won't see her as someone you're supposed to compete with.

Please don't.

She isn't here because I'm still holding onto her.

She's here because she became part of the road that eventually led me to you.

Without those fifteen days...

I might never have learned how deeply I was capable of loving.

Without losing her...

I might never have learned that love also means letting go with kindness.

If you ever find yourself feeling jealous of my past...

remember this.

Every person written here helped shape the man sitting beside you.

But none of them are the reason these pages still exist.

They're here so that, if we ever meet...

you'll know the road that brought me to you.

✦

You'll also meet my mother.

My grandmother.

The people whose absence taught me that grief never truly disappears.

It simply learns how to live quietly beside love.

There are still days when certain songs...

certain scenes...

or certain smells catch me off guard.

If that ever happens while we're together...

please don't worry.

I'm not leaving you for yesterday.

I'm simply visiting people I still carry inside me.

✦

You might notice something else.

Coffee appears a lot.

So do rainy afternoons.

Simple meals.

Random conversations.

Discord.

Games.

Work.

K-dramas.

Ordinary days.

That's because somewhere during all of this...

I realized happiness rarely arrives wearing fireworks.

Most of the time...

it looks like eating fried rice while watching a show.

Or laughing with friends over something incredibly stupid.

Or hearing someone ask,

"Kumain ka na ba?"

If we're lucky...

I hope our life is filled with those ordinary days.

Because I've learned they become extraordinary when shared with the right person.

✦

There's something I should probably confess.

I love deeply.

Sometimes more deeply than I know how to explain.

You'll probably notice it.

I'll remember the little things.

I'll ask if you've eaten.

I'll worry when you're unusually quiet.

I'll celebrate small victories as though they were holidays.

Not because I expect perfection.

Because I've learned that love grows through ordinary moments.

If I ever become overwhelming...

tell me.

I'll listen.

I've learned that love isn't just expressing what's inside your heart.

It's learning how another heart receives it.

✦

More than anything...

I hope you never feel like you arrived too late.

You didn't.

Every chapter before this one wasn't delaying our story.

It was preparing me for it.

The version of me writing these pages still has doubts.

Still gets restless.

Still wonders whether tomorrow will be kind.

But he keeps moving anyway.

Because somewhere inside him...

hope quietly refused to die.

If you're reading this...

then I guess hope was right.

✦

Thank you...

not for saving me.

You were never meant to.

Thank you for meeting the man who learned how to stand on his own.

Thank you for taking the time to know who he used to be.

And thank you...

for becoming part of the chapters that were never written here.

Because those chapters...

we'll write together.

✦

Until then...

welcome.

These pages are the man I was...

before I became
the man you'll know.

— Ralph.
